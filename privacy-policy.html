Information We May Collect

We may collect or receive the following information when you contact us or make an enquiry:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number or WhatsApp contact
  • Your Instagram handle, email address, or other contact details if you contact us through those channels
  • Product enquiry or order details
  • Pickup or delivery date and time requested by you
  • Delivery address, if delivery is requested and confirmed
  • Notes or special requests provided by you
  • Any other information you choose to provide when communicating with us

The website itself does not currently contain a contact form, account registration, or online payment form.

2

Shopping Cart and Local Browser Storage

Our website may use local browser storage, such as localStorage, to remember the products and quantities you add to your shopping cart.

This helps you continue browsing without losing your selected items if you refresh or reopen the website on the same browser.

The shopping cart information stored locally may include:

  • Selected product reference
  • Product quantity

This information is stored on your own device/browser. It is not used for tracking, and it is not sent to us unless you choose to send the generated WhatsApp message.

You can clear this information by:

  • Using the “clear cart” function on the website, if available
  • Clearing your browser’s site data
  • Using private/incognito browsing mode

WhatsApp Enquiries and Order Messages

When you click “Send to WhatsApp”, our website may generate a prefilled WhatsApp message containing the products and quantities selected in your cart.

The message may also include blank fields for you to fill in, such as:

  • Name
  • Contact phone number
  • Pickup or delivery date and time
  • Notes or special requests

The message is only sent to us if you choose to send it through WhatsApp.

Your use of WhatsApp is also subject to WhatsApp’s own privacy policy and terms. We are not responsible for how WhatsApp, Meta, Instagram, or other third-party platforms process your information.
